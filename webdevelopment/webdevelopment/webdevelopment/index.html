Welcome to Your EdTech Toolbox!

This site is designed to offer middle and high school teachers resources for integrating educational technology into instruction. Here, you will be able to access tools, examples, and ready-to-use ideas that can be used in any secondary core content area. The goal is to create accessible and engaging opportunities for learning that showcase student creativity and innovation. If you’re looking for digital in-class activities, project ideas, or inspiration for student-centered learning, then this is the place for you!
