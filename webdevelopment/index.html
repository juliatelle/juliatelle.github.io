Purpose, Goals, & Objectives

This website is designed to support high school English teachers with introducing Shakespeare’s works in ways that are accessible, engaging, and meaningful for struggling readers.

The included strategies and resources focus on building vocabulary, evaluating historical context, and helping students identify and analyze key literary themes in Shakespeare’s plays. Whether they’re teaching Macbeth, Hamlet, Romeo and Juliet, or any other play, teachers will be able to use the resources on this site to prepare students for tackling Shakespearean works.

After engaging with the learning activities featured on this site, teachers will be equipped to help students:

1. Activate their prior knowledge about Shakespeare and drama. 2. Explore the significance of Elizabethan society and its influence on Shakespeare and his plays. 3. Understand and apply essential Shakespearean vocabulary.
